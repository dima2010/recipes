Ingredients:
 - 1/2 pound macaroni
 - 1/4 bacon
 - 2 eggs
 - 2 glass milk
 - 1/4 cheese

 Directions:
 1. Bring water to boil, put macaroni and cook for 10 minutes than drain, put eggs and stear well

 2. Put milk in a sauce pan, bring to boil, put cheese, stear constantly until cheese get melted

 3. Fry bacon until gold and crispy

 4. Serve macaroni, pour sauce and put bacon on the top
